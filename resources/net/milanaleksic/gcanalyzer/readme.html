Heap sizing recommendations

The following recommendations are based on wonderful book by Charlie Hunt and Binu John "Java Performance".

Space Recommendation How to set it up
Total heap 3x to 4x old generation space occupancy after Full garbage collect -Xms and -Xmx
Permanent generation 1.2x to 1.5x permanent generation space occupancy after Full garbage collect -XX:PermSize and -XX:MaxPermSize
Young generation 1x to 1.5x old generation space occupancy after Full garbage collect -Xmn
Total heap 2x to 3x old generation space occupancy after Full garbage collect Implied from total heap size minus the young generation
